Why I chose thisPourquoi ce choix

Why I chose accessible transport.Pourquoi le transport accessible.

My uncle uses a wheelchair. I watched him cancel trips because the taxi never showed, or showed and refused, or showed and wasn't equipped. Years of small frustrations that added up to a quiet decision: don't go.Mon oncle est en fauteuil. Je l'ai vu annuler des voyages parce que le taxi ne venait pas, ou venait sans équipement.

Paris is too good to skip. So in 2014, I trained as a PMR driver, bought a converted van with everything I'd want for him, and started Explorer Taxi.Paris vaut mieux que ça. En 2014, je me suis formé PMR et j'ai créé Explorer Taxi.

Twelve years later it's still just me — and that's the point.Douze ans plus tard, c'est toujours moi seul — et c'est l'idée.
